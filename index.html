What we live and work with

Paintings

Contemporary and modern painting — from expressive abstraction to refined minimalism. Oils, acrylics, mixed media on canvas.

Sculptures

Sculpture in bronze, marble, resin and steel. Limited editions and unique pieces for private and corporate collections.

Design

Microcement, decorative plasters, artificial stone and bespoke textured surfaces. Seamless, contemporary finishes for private residences and commercial spaces.
